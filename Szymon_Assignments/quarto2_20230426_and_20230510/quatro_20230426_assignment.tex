\hypertarget{klan-tv-series}{%
\subsection{KLAN (TV Series)}\label{klan-tv-series}}

\textbf{\emph{Klan}} is a Polish soap opera that premiered on September
22, 1997 on the public TVP1 channel. With more than 4,000 episodes
spanning 25 seasons, the show is the longest-running Polish TV series.
It airs from Monday to Friday at 5.50 p.m. on TVP1.

Klan revolves around the fates of the multi-generation Lubicz family
from Warsaw.
